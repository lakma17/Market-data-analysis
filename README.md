📌 Project Overview

This project analyses declining sales performance and evaluates the effectiveness of marketing campaigns using customer data. The goal is to identify key drivers behind reduced revenue and provide actionable insights to improve engagement, targeting and overall business performance.

❓ Problem Statement

Monthly sales have been declining, with an average drop of ~865 units per month. This project investigates the root causes using a 5 Whys approach, focusing on:

Decreasing product sales Low customer engagement Ineffective marketing strategies

🔍 Key Insights 📉 Sales Trend Sales are declining steadily over time (-865 units/month) Indicates reduced demand and/or ineffective marketing efforts

👥 Customer Engagement Majority of customers are passive and unresponsive Customers exposed to ads show significantly higher engagement Engagement increases with ad exposure (up to ~90% response rate)

💰 Customer Value Customers who respond to campaigns spend 2–3x more High-frequency engagement correlates with higher revenue per customer

🎯 Marketing Effectiveness Digital channels (Instagram, Facebook) outperform traditional methods Ad exposure increases revenue but shows diminishing returns at high frequency Lack of personalisation and targeting reduces campaign impact

🌍 Customer Segmentation Insights Age: 40–69 most responsive Income: Middle to high income groups engage more Education: Graduates & PhDs show higher response rates Geography: Spain, South Africa, and Canada perform best

🛒 Product Insights Strong cross-selling opportunities: Vegetables ↔ Fish Meat ↔ Liqueur Premium products are more influenced by advertising

📊 Customer Segments Segment Description Passive Customers Largest group, low engagement but high total revenue Loyal Customers Smaller group, high spending and strong engagement High Churn Risk Very small group, low engagement and dissatisfied Engaged but Dissatisfied Rare but valuable, potential for retention

⚠️ Key Problems Identified Ineffective targeting strategy Lack of campaign personalisation Ad fatigue reducing responsiveness Over-reliance on a small engaged segment Large untapped passive customer base

🚀 Conclusion

The decline in sales is primarily driven by low engagement among a large passive customer base and ineffective targeting strategies.

Marketing campaigns are highly effective for a smaller, engaged segment, the business must focus on scaling engagement through better personalisation and data-driven targeting to reverse the downward sales trend.

✅ Recommendations 🎯 Improve targeting using customer segmentation 🧠 Personalise campaigns based on behaviour and demographics 🔁 Optimise ad frequency to balance reach and engagement 🛍️ Use cross-selling strategies for related products 💬 Re-engage passive customers with tailored campaigns ⚠️ Address complaints to reduce churn
